> 因为没有被置于「凉爽、干燥、远离阳光」的地方，地球上才有了生命。换句话说，我们是宇宙里发的霉。

> Life on Earth exists because it wasn't placed in a "cool, dry, away from sunlight" location. In other words, humans are the mold that grew in the universe.
